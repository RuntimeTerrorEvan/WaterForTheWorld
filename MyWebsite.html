Water For The World

click a continent to learn about clean water & sanitation

WE need YOUR help! Water is essential to all life. That's why it becomes such a universal problem when it's not available. Families that inhabit countries such as Libya & The Western Sahara are struggling, and it's time to help! Down below, learn about organizations where you can contribute to solving the issue through donations and volunteerwork - and remember, water, is for THE WORLD

As you can see, we've made a difference in the past few decades. However, there is still a long way to go.

Genorosity.org drills wells in highly-vulnrable regions, such as Haiti. Click on the image to visit their website, where you can learn about their mentorship programs and donate money to build wells. You can even decide the region of the well, and stay involved with local partners and officials.

Blood:Water works in Africa to teach improved hygiene practices in households and communities, such as hand-washing, dish-drying racks, garbage pits, and latrines. Click on the image to visit their website, where you can participate through online challenges, donations, and start your own crazy idea to help raise money.

Pure Water For The World engineers innovative water technologies, constructs sanitizing stations in schools and teaches children and families techniques to stay hygienic. Visit their website via the image above to learn about their fundraisers and their unique volunteering activies - in which you get to travel around the world!
